laurels

noun

Show/Hide

1. honor or praise awarded for an achievement.

"he has rightly won laurels for this brilliantly perceptive first novel"

2. an aromatic evergreen shrub related to the bay tree, several kinds of which form forests in tropical and warm countries.

3. the foliage of the bay tree woven into a wreath or crown and worn on the head as an emblem of victory or mark of honor in classical times.
