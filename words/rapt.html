rapt

adjective

Show/Hide

1. completely fascinated by what one is seeing or hearing.

"Andrew looked at her, rapt"

2. indicating or characterized by a state of fascination.

"they listened with rapt attention"

3. filled with an intense and pleasurable emotion; enraptured.

"she shut her eyes and seemed rapt with desire"
