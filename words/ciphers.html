ciphers

noun

Show/Hide

1. a secret or disguised way of writing; a code.

"he was writing cryptic notes in a cipher"

2. a person or thing of no importance, especially a person who does the bidding of others and seems to have no will of their own.

"journalists are not mere interchangeable ciphers in the propaganda battle"
