proselytizing

noun

Show/Hide

the action of attempting to convert someone from one religion, belief, or opinion to another.

"no amount of proselytizing was going to change their minds"

1. proselytize (verb): convert or attempt to convert (someone) from one religion, belief, or opinion to another.

"the program did have a tremendous evangelical effect, proselytizing many"

2. advocate or promote (a belief or course of action).
