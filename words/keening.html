keening

noun

Show/Hide

1. the action of wailing in grief for a dead person.

"the keening of grieving mothers tore into an otherwise silent afternoon"

2. an eerie wailing sound.

"the keening of the cold night wind"

3. (adjective) (of a sound) prolonged and high-pitched, typically in a way that expresses grief or sorrow.
